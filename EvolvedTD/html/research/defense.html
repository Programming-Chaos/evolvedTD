Overview of Defense

Defense is the capacity or ability to resist or avoid damage or injury. This aids in an organisms survival by making it more difficult for them to be killed by predators or the environment. Defense has evolved many different ways in nature, including a hard surface or shell covering the organism, sharp points such as quills or spines, and blending into the surrounding area through camouflage are all examples of defense mechanisms.
